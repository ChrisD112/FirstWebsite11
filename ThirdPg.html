The Capitals

Capitals Today!

The current Washington Capitals feature a strong mix of veteran leadership and young talent. Alex Ovechkin continues to lead the team, providing scoring ability and experience. Newer additions and developing players have brought more depth to the lineup, helping the team stay competitive. Solid coaching and improved defensive play have also contributed to their steady performance. With a balanced roster, the Capitals remain a team capable of making a strong push in the playoffs. Roster

Hershey Bears

The Hershey Bears, one of the oldest and most storied franchises in minor-league hockey, serve as the Washington Capitals' AHL affiliate. The two organizations recently extended their partnership through the 2029-30 season, reflecting a strong and stable relationship. Hershey has enjoyed major success in recent years, winning back-to-back Calder Cups in 2023 and 2024. Many players and coaches move between Hershey and Washington, making the Bears a crucial development pipeline for the Capitals. With their history of winning and consistent player growth, the Hershey Bears remain an essential part of the Capitals' organizational success.

Capitals Prospects

The Washington Capitals have a promising crop of young talent in their system. At the top is Ryan Leonard, a powerful forward recently signed from Boston College who already saw some NHL action. Ivan Miroshnichenko showed off some offensive flair in the AHL with Hershey and could push for a full-time NHL role, while Cole Hutson is a highly skilled defenseman from Boston University who stands out with poise and playmaking ability. Meanwhile, Lynden Lakovic is a big, skilled winger from the WHL with size and a scoring touch that could make him a future top-six forward.
